VR



GREAT VR TAKES A GREAT GPU

Virtual reality delivers some of the most immersive experiences in PC gaming. But getting VR right is hard. With effective display resolutions at 4K and growing, minimum refresh rates of 90 Hz, and razor-thin latency tolerances, VR is one of the world’s most demanding PC applications and requires the world’s most powerful GPUs. GeForce RTX™ GPUs from NVIDIA give you the performance to power top titles on the latest VR headsets. Plus, they come packed with technology to crank up settings for truly mind-blowing, immersive gameplay.
